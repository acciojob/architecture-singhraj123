Urban Architecture

Urban architecture focuses on designing buildings in cities where space is limited. It emphasizes functionality, sustainability, and aesthetic appeal. Skyscrapers, apartments, and commercial complexes are examples of urban architecture.

Classical Architecture

Classical architecture is inspired by ancient Greek and Roman designs. It features columns, symmetry, and detailed craftsmanship. This style represents tradition, culture, and timeless beauty in construction.
